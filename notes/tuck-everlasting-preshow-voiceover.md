# Tuck Everlasting — Pre-Show Voice-Over Script

---

*[Warm, welcoming tone — friendly and a little playful]*

Good evening, and welcome to Accolade Theatre! We are so glad you're here tonight for our production of **Tuck Everlasting**.

---

Before we get started, we have a special request — and yes, this is the one time we're actually *asking* you to take out your phone.

Go ahead. Take it out. Take a great photo of this beautiful theatre, snap a selfie with your crew, or grab a shot of the stage.

Then **share it!** Tag us on social media — we'd love to see your night through your eyes.

And once you've posted… **silence that phone, flip it face down, and tuck it away** — *pun absolutely intended* — so everyone around you can lose themselves in the story.

---

A few quick reminders before we begin:

**Tonight's show includes a brief intermission** — or as we say here in Texas… **Halftime.** So pace yourself on those concessions.

---

And now, some exciting news from Accolade Theatre:

**The Lion King** — May 15th through 17th. Tickets are on sale right now! Visit **accoladetheatre.org** and head to the Tickets page for your link.

Coming this summer — **The Entire American Revolution in 40 Minutes or Less** — June 11th through 13th. Tickets on sale soon!

Calling all performers — **Newsies auditions** are coming up **May 18th and 19th!** Visit **accoladetheatre.org** for details on how to prepare and sign up.

And mark your calendars for **June 19th** — it's the **Accolade Awards and Season Six Announcement Night!** Hold that date and keep an eye on our website and social media for all the details. You will not want to miss this one.

---

*[Settle back in]*

All right, friends — the curtain is about to rise on an extraordinary story about living, loving, and what it really means to be alive.

Sit back, soak it in, and enjoy… **Tuck Everlasting.**
